 Figure 40b — Feature-by-feature distribution comparison across segments (second set of variables). Figures 40a–b drill back down from the summary level (radars, normalised deviations) to the raw distributional level, showing how each individual feature is distributed *within* each segment rather than just comparing segment averages. This step matters because an average can hide a great deal — two segments could have the same mean spend on a category while one is tightly concentrated around that mean and the other is bimodal (e.g., “half the segment buys a lot, half buys none”).
