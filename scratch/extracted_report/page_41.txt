 Figure 38 — Each segment's key features expressed as a deviation (z-score / percentage difference) from the overall population average. Figure 38 is the analytical heart of the characterization work — it is the chart from which every one of the eight segment names is ultimately derived. By re-expressing each segment's averages as “how many standard deviations (or what % difference) from the population mean”, the notebook removes the scale differences between categories (alcohol spend and video-game spend live on very different absolute scales) and makes the *distinctive* features of each segment jump out visually. This is the chart equivalent of asking “forget what this group spends in total — what does this group spend *unusually much or unusually little* on, compared to everyone else?”, which is precisely the question a marketing team needs answered before designing a targeted campaign. 7.6 Radar profile summaries
