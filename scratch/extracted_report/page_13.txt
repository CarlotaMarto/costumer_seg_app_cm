It therefore engineers an annualised spend variant alongside the raw lifetime figures, and also prepares log-transformed and share-of-total versions, exporting the absolute, untransformed feature set as the primary clustering input while explicitly deferring the scaling decision to the clustering notebook (“scaling is treated as a modelling choice in the clustering notebook, where different scalers can be compared on the same final feature set”).
